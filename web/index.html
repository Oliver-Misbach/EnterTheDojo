ENTER THE DOJO

Take on a gauntlet of skilled martial artists in a test of your combat prowess! Become a true master as you use your variety of attacks to best your opponents. Experience the detailed animations that make each punch worthy of a black belt warrior. But beware of the final boss, who knows every technique you do. Aim for the high score by perfecting your abilities in Enter the Dojo!
